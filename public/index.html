About us

We’ve played it, we’ve done it, we’ve come to love it as children and now, we embrace it as part of our life. Many years have passed but ultimately the purpose hasn’t changed: sport is first and foremost about having fun. Sharing sport moments enrich our lives in many ways. However, finding people to play with isn’t always a piece of cake.

Often, too often for us at Pulsebond, we struggled finding people to do sport with. That’s why we decided to do something about it. We are on a mission to connect people locally around the sports that they’re into. We take care of the boring part of sport, you keep the fun. Spread the word: Come out, make it Pulse, together.
